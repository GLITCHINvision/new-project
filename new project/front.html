🍰 Disclaimer

At RizzBakes, every cake is baked with love and ethics in mind. All our goodies are 100% vegan – no dairy, no eggs, no honey, and absolutely no animal-derived ingredients. 🌿

We source our ingredients responsibly to support a healthier planet and kinder choices. Our kitchen follows strict hygiene and allergen-safe practices. Please let us know in advance if you have nut, soy, or gluten allergies.

🕊️ Eating vegan isn't just about food – it's about compassion, sustainability, and delicious change. With every bite, you’re supporting animals, the environment, and your own well-being.

For any questions, special requests, or ingredients list – feel free to reach out. We’re here to spread joy, one cruelty-free cake at a time! 🎂✨
